Top Foods in Switzerland

Rösti

Rösti is a traditional Swiss dish of fried potato pancakes that's often considered the country's national dish: !

Fondue

This iconic Swiss dish features melted cheese served with bread for dipping. A must-try for cheese lovers!

Alplermagronen

Alpine macaroni (älplermagronen or alpen macaroni) is essentially Swiss mac and cheese with potatoes. Macaroni and cubed potatoes collectively drown in a decadent cheese sauce!.

Schnitze

Schnipo (a schnitzel and fried potato combination) is quite popular. The Rahmschnitzel version is made with either veal or pork and topped with a cream sauce, sometimes including mushrooms.!

Birchermüesli

It's popular for breakfast as it gives you a healthy boost of vitamins, proteins and healthy starchy ingredients for a great start into the morning!

Zuger Kirschtorte

Swiss layer cake that consists of layers of nut-meringue, sponge cake and butter cream, and is flavoured with the cherry brandy kirschwasser.!
